1000-Introduction


INTRODUCTION TO SymbOS Control Panel

The control panel in SymbOS is used to setup your general settings in SymbOS.

1100-Feature overview


FEATURES

In the control panel you can choose the what to setup or to change.

The list of settings you can change in the control panel:

-Date and Time Settings
-Display settings, like resolution, screensave and background
-Load other font types -Add or remove desktop items when advanged desktop is not enabled
-Keyboard settings. You can choose keys or re-map them
-Mouse settings, like speed settings
-Mass Storage settings. To attach and choose drive letters
-General SymbOS system Settings

2000-Using the control panel


Using the contol panel
Choose a specific item in the control panel if you want to change settings for that item.
Don't forget to save your settings in the filemenu!
